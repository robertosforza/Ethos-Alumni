Who we are

ETHOS is an observatory on public ethics founded by Sebastiano Maffettone at the Luiss Business School. The Alumni Network brings together "we, the Ethos people" — students, alumni, and collaborators — with the goal of maintaining connections and creating long-term value and social capital.
